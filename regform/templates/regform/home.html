Admission Pathway

General Requirement

The procedure of new intake registration as stated below.

  • Fill in the online registration form.
  • Receive LoA (Letter of Acceptance) in 3 days after form submission via E-mail.
  • Payment through Finance Bureau via telegraphic transfer.
  • Receive official payment receipt through LINE or Whatsapp by our Education Consultant Staff.
  • Required data to be submitted upon September 2021 or informed by Education Consultant.

For more detailed procedure, please contact one of our Education Consultant Staff for further instruction.

Data Entry Requirement

The document that is needed to be submitted:

  • 12 Recent colored photographs red background, black tie & black blazer
    1. 2x3 = 4 pcs
    2. 3x4 = 4 pcs
    3. 4x6 = 4 pcs
  • Photographs in hardcopy
  • 2 copies of family card
  • 2 copies of ID card
  • 2 copies of birth certificate
  • 1 pcs folder map
    1. Red for Management
    2. Yellow for Accounting
    3. Navy Blue for Information System
    4. Soft Blue for Entrepreneurship
    5. Brown for International Trade
    6. Green for Hospitality Management
  • 4 copies of Legalized IJAZAH (Indonesia's Official Graduation School Certificate)
  • 4 copies of Legalized SKHU (Optional)
  • 4 copies of Legalized SKHUN

All of the documents must be completed in folder colored as a major that you choose.
